Game "Painy Stairs"

Team:Oleh Volchkov,Taras Hirnak,Ivan Sharko,Pavlo Yuskiv

We are making a video game for android in the Unity engine. We are using C# language for scripting. Idea of the game is very simple. Player controls character, which could only jump upstairs or downstairs. While jumping up, stairs are being generated, but in the same time stairs below the character are falling down. Player should avoid falling obstacles by dodging them. The game will be over, if the character collides with an obstacle or fall down.

Tracefull

Team:Inesa Hermaniuk and Nazar Mamchur

We are working on Android app, which helps to find interesting events in your city not far from you exactly on a map. User can join the event, having seen its venue, invite friends, see other members and ratings among users. In addition, you can create your own events and invite people. You may send invitations to your friends and wait for their acceptance or refusal or may make the event available for everyone.
